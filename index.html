Projects - Work

EPC Analysis of Warm Welcome Spaces

Warm Welcome is a scheme run by the Good Faith Partnership. It partners with a variety of public spaces to provide a 'warm' and 'welcome' environment, promoting community belonging and connection. With ongoing and future proposals regarding energy requirements/net zero targets, energy efficiency, and the measure of it, has been a major theme in the charity sector within my experience. I analysed the EPC data for the Warm Welcome spaces and created a streamlit app to enable partners and stakeholders to interact with and visualise the findings.

Cost Calculator

Part of the assessment of grantees involved calculating the value for money of each project, involving various metrics about the project. Often I would recieve ad-hoc requests for the figure from internal and external stakeholders. At the time I was working on my web development skills and created a cost calculator on CodePen. I gave the link to external grant managers to save time and allow them to use it at their convenience.

Projects - Non Work

Scavenger Hunt App

Someone I knew had made an instagram story about how much time and difficulty they had experienced in planning their annual birthday 'scavenger hunt'. At the time, I was creating Streamlit apps both to develop my personal skills and to test for use within the grants and loans teams. I got in touch with this person and ended up making them a Streamlit app for their hunt. It was a huge success as a last minute solution (it was 2 days from the scheduled hunt) and I recieved many compliments for attendees!
